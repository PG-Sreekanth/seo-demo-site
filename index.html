What are the benefits of eating millets compared to other grains?

Millets have a lower glycemic index compared to refined rice and wheat, making them an excellent choice for managing blood sugar levels. They are also rich in nutrients like calcium, iron, and protein, which are essential for maintaining healthy bones, skin, and hair. Additionally, millets are gluten-free and have a higher fiber content, making them an excellent option for individuals with gluten intolerance or digestive issues.
